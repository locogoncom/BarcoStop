Privacy Policy - BarcoStop

Effective date: 2026-03-29

BarcoStop ("the App") is committed to protecting user privacy and security. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and what controls users have.

1. Data controller

  • App: BarcoStop
  • Contact: support@barcostop.app

2. Data we process

Depending on App usage, we may process:

  • Account data: name, email address, password hash.
  • Profile data: avatar, bio, boat-related profile details published by the user.
  • Service usage data: trips, bookings, chat messages, favorites, donations, and minimal technical service logs.
  • Safety/moderation data: user blocks and abuse reports in chat.
  • Image files: only when a user chooses to upload an avatar.

We do not request camera, microphone, or contacts permissions.

3. Purpose of processing

We use data to:

  • Create and manage accounts.
  • Enable secure authentication.
  • Provide core features (trips, bookings, chat).
  • Prevent abuse and perform moderation (blocking/reporting).
  • Improve service reliability and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal basis

We process personal data based on:

  • Performance of the requested service.
  • Legitimate interest in security, fraud prevention, and moderation.
  • Legal compliance where applicable.

5. Data retention

We keep data while the account is active or as needed for operations, security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance. Data is then deleted or anonymized according to internal policy.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only when required for:

  • Technical providers hosting infrastructure.
  • Legal compliance or valid authority requests.
  • Protection of user safety and platform rights.

7. Security

We apply technical and organizational safeguards, including:

  • Access controls and authentication.
  • Expiring session tokens.
  • API security policies.
  • In-chat anti-abuse controls (blocking/reporting).
  • No cleartext traffic on Android.

8. User rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data and exercise applicable rights by contacting support@barcostop.app.

9. Children

The App is not intentionally directed to children without legal supervision. If improperly processed data is detected, it will be removed.

10. Policy updates

We may update this policy. The latest version and effective date will always be published.
